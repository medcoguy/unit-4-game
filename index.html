*******INSTRUCTIONS******* "Magic Number" shows a random number.

Each spider has a random point value.

User should click on each spider to determine its value, then continue clicking on the spider/s whose value will give them a "total" equal to the Magic Number.

The player wins if their total score matches the Magic Number.

The player loses if their score goes above the Magic Number.
